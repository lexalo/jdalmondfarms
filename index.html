Health Fact

Almonds are one of the most nutrient-dense tree-nut. One ounce of almonds (about 23) contains approximately 160 calories and only 1 gram of saturated fat. The same handful is also an excellent source of vitamin E and magnesium, a good source of protein and potassium without cholesterol.
